# 🏆 Prime Minister's Youth Laptop Scheme 2025

## Award Overview

I was selected as a recipient of the **Prime Minister's Youth Laptop Scheme 2025**, a merit-based initiative by the Government of Pakistan aimed at empowering outstanding university students through access to modern technology.

This award recognizes academic performance, dedication to learning, and the effective use of digital tools to support higher education, research, and innovation.

---

## 📌 Award Details

- **Award:** Prime Minister's Youth Laptop Scheme 2025
- **Issued By:** Government of Pakistan
- **Year:** 2025
- **Category:** Merit-Based Academic Award

---

## 🎯 Significance

Receiving this award has enabled me to:

- Enhance my technical and programming skills.
- Perform advanced simulations using MATLAB and ETAP.
- Develop embedded systems projects using ESP32 and Arduino.
- Complete internationally recognized certifications in Machine Learning, Python, and Solar Energy.
- Support my Final Year Project on an off-grid solar inverter with smart control and transient stability enhancement.
